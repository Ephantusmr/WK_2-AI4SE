MedAssist AI is an AI-powered clinical assistant designed to support nurses, community health workers, and general practitioners in diagnosing common illnesses, suggesting evidence-based treatments, and recommending referrals.

With an ML-driven approach, MedAssist AI aims to reduce dependency on overburdened specialists, especially in rural and underserved regions where medical expertise is scarce.
